Features:

- Cross-device synchronizations.
- No middleware, all of the logic in the application.
- Upload photos & videos.
- Search by type (Photo, Video, Screeshot) or by full name.
- Group content by date.
- End-to-End Encryption: every thing is encrypted/decrypted on the device itself.
- Sharing.
- Media info. (Geolocation, size ...)
- Caching for faster retrieval.
- Resume download and upload of large files (videos)
- Works only on private repositories for added security.
